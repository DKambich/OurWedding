Our Story

Get caught up on our lives before the big day!

  • Met at Purdue

    August 17, 2017

    We met at lunch (or dinner, depending on which of us you ask) during the second day of orientation. It all started when Daniel asked what her name was...

  • Started Our Relationship

    September 17, 2017

    Only a month after meeting, we knew we were right for one another. After watching the movie Jaws one night in a crowded dorm room with our friends, we started our relationship.

  • Graduated From Purdue

    May 15, 2021

    After so many great memories together at Purdue, we finished our time at our home for four years. With college finished, we both were thinking about what would come next.

  • Moved to Minneapolis

    June 19, 2021

    After graduating from Purdue University, we continued our adventure together up north to Minnesota. Both of us started our new careers in suburbs of Minneapolis.

  • Got Engaged

    September 17, 2021

    After four fun-filled and happy years of dating, Daniel proposed on our anniversary, which we will continue on our wedding day.

Frequently Asked Questions

This section will be updated frequently so please check back often for updates and additional information!

What measures are being taken to keep everyone safe and healthy?

We are asking that all of our guests are vaccinated/boosted before attending our wedding. If you are unable to get vaccinated and over the age of 2, we request that you wear a mask for the duration of the event. Thank you for doing your part to keep everyone safe. We will continue to monitor this situation and update as necessary.

What should I wear?

We are asking our guests to dress in semi-formal or cocktail attire for our wedding. Both the ceremony and reception venue are indoors.

How will I know who is invited?

We politely request that only the people listed on your invitation be present. If you have questions, please feel free to reach out to us!

Where will the ceremony and reception be taking place?

We will be having a full Catholic Mass ceremony at St. Ambrose Catholic Church (2801 Lincoln Street Anderson, IN 46016). The cocktail hour and reception will be taking place at the Rangeline Center (1405 North Rangeline Road Anderson, IN 46012) just across town.

I have questions that were not answered here! What is the best way to get in touch with you?

You can email us at questions@abigaildanielwedding.com or you can text us if you have either of our numbers.
